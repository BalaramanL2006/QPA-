Sample Question Paper for Testing

EASY QUESTIONS:
1. Define photosynthesis
2. List the types of ecosystems
3. What is the water cycle?
4. Identify the organs in the human digestive system
5. Name the planets in our solar system
6. State the law of conservation of energy
7. Write the chemical formula for glucose

MEDIUM QUESTIONS:
8. Explain the process of cellular respiration
9. Describe the structure and function of mitochondria
10. Summarize the carbon cycle
11. Discuss the importance of biodiversity in nature
12. Illustrate the nitrogen cycle
13. Outline the steps of photosynthesis

HARD QUESTIONS:
14. Analyze the impact of deforestation on climate change
15. Evaluate the effectiveness of renewable energy sources compared to fossil fuels
16. Justify the necessity of endangered species protection programs
17. Compare the theories of evolution proposed by different scientists
18. Criticize the limitations of traditional farming methods
19. How can you synthesis knowledge from different fields to solve environmental problems?

MIXED DIFFICULTY (FOR REAL-WORLD TESTING):
20. Define primary and secondary succession in ecosystems
21. Explain the relationship between evolution and natural selection
22. Analyze the causes and consequences of biodiversity loss
23. Describe the role of microorganisms in soil formation
24. Evaluate forest conservation strategies in developing countries
25. Compare photosynthesis in C3 and C4 plants
26. Justify the use of genetic engineering in agriculture
27. Criticize the impact of urbanization on natural habitats
28. Name three types of renewable energy sources
29. List the factors affecting enzyme activity
